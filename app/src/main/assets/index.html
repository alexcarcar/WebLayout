America's National Parks

America's national parks are one of the country's greatest resources.

History of the Parks

The first national park was Yellowstone, created during the term of President Ulysses S. Grant in 1872. The Park Service was established by an Act of Congress signed by President Woodrow Wilson on August 25, 1916.
